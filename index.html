Objective

Winning Level One

  • Move Up / Move Down / Move Left / Move Right Using the controls listed above
  • Do Not collide with any entity apart from a blackhole
  • You can collide with the blackhole, as it doesn't reset play
    Note Colliding with the blackhole, will move you uncontrollably, but you can use it to steer clear of enemy entities. Use it well.
  • Reach the space where the Imperilled Udacity shuttle is located.
    NoteYou do not have to go to the imperilled Udacity space shuttle's co-ordinates.
    What you can do, Is use a guided automatic docking mechanism.
    • Reach the Y coordinates of the imperilled Udacity space shuttle anywhere along the X axis
    • Then Rotate Right Only, to a degree between 90 to 100
    • You would then be guided automatically to dock with the imperilled Udacity space shuttle
  • Once you are superposed with the imperilled Udacity shuttle, and Docked, by rotating right to an angle between 90 to 100 degrees, begin returning to the lower most safe space zone
  • On successfully completing the above, you have won the level

Time constraints

  • You have three minutes to complete the level
  • If you do not complete by three minutes, the game resets hard.
